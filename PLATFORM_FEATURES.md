# 🌟 Shared Wealth International Platform Features

## Overview

The Shared Wealth International platform is now a comprehensive networking and collaboration hub where users can manage their profiles, companies, create events, and engage in community discussions. This document outlines all the features and how to use them.

## 🏗️ **Core Features**

### 1. **User Profile Management** (`/profile`)

**What it does:**
- Complete user profile management
- Personal information editing
- Company associations display
- Activity tracking
- Account settings

**Key Features:**
- ✅ **Personal Information**: Name, bio, position, contact details
- ✅ **Social Links**: Website, LinkedIn, Twitter
- ✅ **Company Associations**: View all companies you're part of
- ✅ **Activity Feed**: Track your platform engagement
- ✅ **Account Settings**: Privacy, notifications, password management

**How to use:**
1. Navigate to `/profile`
2. Click "Edit Profile" to modify your information
3. Fill in your details and save changes
4. View your company associations and activity

### 2. **Company Management** (`/companies`)

**What it does:**
- Create and manage companies
- Post company updates and news
- Track company analytics
- Manage company information

**Key Features:**
- ✅ **Company Creation**: Create new companies in the network
- ✅ **Company Editing**: Update company information and details
- ✅ **News & Updates**: Post company news, events, and updates
- ✅ **Company Analytics**: View engagement metrics
- ✅ **Multi-Company Support**: Manage multiple company associations

**How to use:**
1. Navigate to `/companies`
2. Click "Create Company" to add a new company
3. Fill in company details (name, description, sector, location, website)
4. Use "Post Update" to share company news
5. View analytics and manage company information

### 3. **Events System** (`/events`)

**What it does:**
- Create and manage events
- Join events as participants
- Virtual and in-person event support
- Event categorization and filtering

**Key Features:**
- ✅ **Event Creation**: Create workshops, conferences, meetups, webinars
- ✅ **Virtual Events**: Support for online events with meeting links
- ✅ **Event Management**: Edit, update, and manage your events
- ✅ **Event Discovery**: Browse upcoming, ongoing, and past events
- ✅ **Participant Management**: Track event participation
- ✅ **Event Categories**: Filter by event type and status

**Event Types:**
- **Workshop**: Hands-on learning sessions
- **Conference**: Large-scale professional gatherings
- **Meetup**: Informal networking events
- **Webinar**: Online educational sessions

**How to use:**
1. Navigate to `/events`
2. Click "Create Event" to start a new event
3. Fill in event details (title, description, dates, location)
4. Choose event type and set participant limits
5. Add virtual meeting link if it's an online event
6. Browse and join other events

### 4. **Discussion Forum** (`/forum`)

**What it does:**
- Community discussions and Q&A
- Topic categorization
- Reply and engagement system
- Search and filtering capabilities

**Key Features:**
- ✅ **Topic Creation**: Start discussions on various subjects
- ✅ **Category System**: Organize topics by category
- ✅ **Reply System**: Engage with community discussions
- ✅ **Search & Filter**: Find relevant discussions quickly
- ✅ **User Engagement**: Track views, replies, and participation

**Forum Categories:**
- **General Discussion**: General community topics
- **Business & Strategy**: Business-related discussions
- **Technology**: Tech and innovation topics
- **Sustainability**: Environmental and sustainability discussions
- **Events & Networking**: Event-related discussions

**How to use:**
1. Navigate to `/forum`
2. Click "New Topic" to start a discussion
3. Choose a category and write your topic
4. Browse existing topics and add replies
5. Use search and filters to find relevant discussions

## 🎯 **User Journey Examples**

### **New User Onboarding:**
1. **Sign Up** → Create account at `/auth`
2. **Complete Profile** → Fill in personal information at `/profile`
3. **Join/Create Company** → Associate with existing company or create new one at `/companies`
4. **Explore Community** → Browse events and forum discussions
5. **Engage** → Join events, participate in discussions, share updates

### **Company Representative:**
1. **Manage Company** → Update company information and post updates
2. **Create Events** → Host workshops, webinars, or networking events
3. **Engage Community** → Participate in forum discussions
4. **Track Analytics** → Monitor company engagement and event participation

### **Community Member:**
1. **Browse Events** → Find relevant events to attend
2. **Join Discussions** → Participate in forum conversations
3. **Network** → Connect with other members through events and discussions
4. **Share Knowledge** → Contribute to community discussions

## 🔧 **Technical Features**

### **Database Structure:**
- **Profiles**: Enhanced user profiles with social links
- **Companies**: Company information and management
- **Events**: Event creation, management, and participation
- **Forum**: Topics, replies, and engagement tracking
- **Company Posts**: News and updates from companies

### **Security & Permissions:**
- **Row Level Security (RLS)**: Database-level access control
- **User Authentication**: Secure login and session management
- **Role-based Access**: Different permissions for different user types
- **Data Validation**: Server-side validation for all inputs

### **Real-time Features:**
- **Live Updates**: Real-time notifications and updates
- **Instant Messaging**: Real-time forum replies
- **Event Updates**: Live event status and participant updates
